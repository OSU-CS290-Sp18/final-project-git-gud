THE HERO WE NEED

YOU ARE A NEW ADVENTURER ON A QUEST AWAY FROM YOUR HOME VILLAGE. IN THE SMALL TOWN OF BRETHERTON, YOU STOP AT A TAVERN FOR SOME MUCH NEEDED FOOD AND REST. YOU PLAN TO SPEND A NIGHT THERE AFTER FINISHING YOUR SUPPER. SUDDENLY, A COMMOTION BURSTS THROUGH THE DOOR.

A RAGGED FARMER SEEKS AID. HE EXCLAIMS THAT A GROUP OF MEN RAIDED HIS CART THAT HAD MOST OF HIS FOOD, MONEY, AND PERSONAL BELONGINGS. WHAT DO YOU DO?

A. GO WITH THE FARMER

B. STAY AT THE TAVERN

THE CART

YOU AND THE FARMER FIND THE BANDITS WITH FULL ARMS AND HEAVY POCKETS, PREPARING TO LEAVE THE SCENE OF THE CRIME. THERE ARE THREE OF THEM, EACH MORE BRUTISH THAN THE LAST. THE FARMER SUBTLY COWERS AWAY. YOUR HAND WARILY HOVERS THE HILT OF YOUR SWORD. YOU MUST ACT BEFORE THE BANDITS GATHER THEIR WITS AS A GROUP. WHAT DO YOU DO?

A. TRY TO FIGHT

B. TRY TO SMOOTH-TALK

C. TRY TO INTIMIDATE

WHAT A PITY

IN THE FACE OF HARDSHIP, YOU FAIL TO RISE TO THE OCCASION. NOT EVERYONE CAN BE A HERO, IT WOULD SEEM.

AN UNFAIR FIGHT

THE BANDITS ATTACK FIRST. THEY HAVE NUMBERS ADVANTAGE. TAKE ACTION.

A. PROTECT THE FARMER

B. GO ON THE OFFENSIVE

C. RUN AWAY

A TRAGIC FINALE

THE BATTLE WAS LONG AND DIFFICULT. YOU SUSTAINED MULTIPLE DANGEROUS WOUNDS. THE FARMER WAS SLAIN IN THE CHAOS, AND LYING WITH HIM NOW ARE THE BODIES OF THE THREE BANDITS. THIS IS NOT QUITE WHAT YOU IMAGINED HEROISM TO BE.

ARE YOU SATISFIED?

YOU SUCCESSFULLY DEFEND THE FARMER FROM THE BANDITS, SLAYING ONE AND SENDING THE OTHERS PACKIKNG WITH INJURY. MANY OF THE FARMER'S BELONGINGS HAVE BEEN RECOVERED, BUT THEY ESCAPED WITH ALL OF THE GOLD. THE FARMER HAS NO REWARD BUT HIS THANKS. WHAT COMES NEXT?

A. GO BACK TO THE TAVERN

B. CHASE AFTER THE BANDITS

AND STAY OUT

YOU MAKE YOURSELF APPEAR AS LARGE AND DANGEROUS AS POSSIBLE. UNSHEATING YOUR SWORD, YOU KEEP SAFE DISTANCE AND CALMY WARN THE BANDITS TO LEAVE. SENSING THE COOL DEMEANOR OF THE NEWCOMER, THE BANDITS REALIZE THAT IT IS IN THEIR BEST INTEREST TO LISTEN. THEY KEEP ONLY WHAT IS ALREADY IN THEIR POCKETS, TURN TAIL AND RUN.

THE MYSTERIOUS LAIR

YOU TRACK THE BANDITS USING A FAINT BLOOD TRAIL FROM THEIR INJURIES. NEAR THE BOTTOM OF A CLIFF FACE, THE TRAIL ABRUPTLY ENDS. YOU PATIENTLY HIDE NEARBY, AND AFTER A COUPLE OF HOURS A HIDDEN DOORWAY OPENS OUT OF THE WALL, LETTING A GROUP OF MEN OUT. IT APPEARS TO BE SOME KIND OF BANDIT LAIR. WHAT IS YOUR MOVE?

A. CHARGE INTO THE CAMP WITH YOUR SWORD DRAWN

B. INVESTIGATE THE SURROUNDING AREA AND LOOK FOR A WAY IN

A BOLD MOVE

BLURRING THE LINES BETWEEN HEROISM, BOLDNESS, AND STUPIDITY, YOU CHARGE INTO THE UNKNOWN. THIS WAS A BIGGER OPERATION THAN YOU IMAGINED, AND YOU ARE OVERWHELEMED BY YOUR ENEMIES.

SMOKE AND MIRRORS

YOU EXPLORE THE SURROUNDING AREA. YOU FIND AN ISOLATED SCOUT AND FORCE HIM TO RELINQUISH HIS CLOTHING, INCLUDING AN INSIGNIA SPECIFIC TO THE APPARENT GANG ACTIVITY. SWAPPING HIS CLOTHING FOR YOUR OWN, YOU LURK NEARBY FOR THE NEXT PATROL TO LEAVE THE CAMP AND SNEAK INSIDE. YOU ARE ABLE TO MOVE THROUGHOUT THE CAMP COMPLETELY UNNOTICED. AFTER A SHORT WHILE, YOU LOCATE THE LEADER'S QUARTERS. WITH YOUR HEART RACING, YOU UNSHEATE YOUR SWORD AND HOLD IT TO THE BANDIT LORD'S BACK.

A. KILL HIM

B. BARGAIN FOR THE FARMER'S BELONGINGS

C. EXTORT HIM FOR GOLD

SOUND THE BELLS

WITH HIS DYING BREATH, THE BANDIT LEADER TRIGGERS AN ALARM. WITH INTENSE UPROAR, THE BANDITS THUNDER TOWARD THEIR LEADER'S QUARTERS. YOUR FATE IS SEALED. YOU STAND AT THE READY IN THE DOORWAY.

A SATISFYING MEAL

THE BANDIT LEADER, WITHOUT MUCH CHOICE, TELLS YOU WHERE THE STORAGE AREA IS SO THAT YOU CAN RETRIEVE THE FARMER'S GOODS. YOU LEAVE WITH ALL OF THE FARMER'S GOLD (AND SOME EXTRA FOR YOURSELF OF COURSE). UPON RETURNING TO THE TOWN, YOU RETURN THE FARMER'S GOODS AND HEAD FOR THE TAVERN. AFTER A LONG DAY, THERE IS NOTHING LIKE WARM FOOD AND COLD DRINK. JUST ANOTHER DAY FOR A HERO.

A LIFTIME OF RICHES

WITH HIS LIFE ON THE LINE, THE BANDIT LORD EVENTUALLY MUST GIVE IN TO YOUR MONETARY DEMANDS. YOU WALK HIM AT SWORDPOINT TO A VAULT, WHICH HE UNLOCKS. YOU FORCE HIM TO STUFF YOUR PACK WITH ALL OF THE GOLD THAT CAN FEASIBLY FIT. ONCE COMPLETE, YOU WALK THE LEADER BACK TOWARD THE DUNGEON ENTRANCE. ONCE YOU RECOGNIZE YOUR ENVIRONMENT, YOU LEAVE HIM BEHIND AND MAKE A DASH FOR THE EXIT.

FROM YOUR FIRST QUEST, YOU MANAGED TO OBTAIN A LIFETIME-WORTH OF RICHES. ONCE BACK IN THE TOWN, YOU SPEAK NOTHING OF YOUR NEWFOUND WEALTH. YOU LEAVE A SMALL POUCH OF GOLD ON THE FARMER'S DOORSTEP AND HEAD TO THE TAVERN TO ENJOY THE CHEAPEST PINT OF YOUR LIFE.

BACK TO THE QUIET LIFE

THIS CLOSE BRUSH WITH DEATH LEFT YOU WITH NO FURTHER DESIRE FOR BLOODSHED. YOUR ADVENTUROUS THIRST HAS BEEN QUENCHED, AND NOW YOUR LITERAL THIRST AS YOU DWELL ON THESE EVENTS OVER A BEER AT THE TAVERN'S MAIN BAR.
